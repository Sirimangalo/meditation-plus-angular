Meditation

The Meditation tab allows you to submit a meditation session to the server and view sessions currently in progress. The list of sessions also shows recently completed sessions below active ones. Above the list there is a graph with the numbers 0-23 below it. The numbers represent hours of the day, and the current hour by UTC standard time is highlighted in yellow. The green bar graphs represent total minutes of meditation per hour (UTC) for the last month, meant to give an idea of when you are most likely to find other meditators online.

Alarm sound

  • Please note that you need to set an alarm sound in your profile first.
  • Please note that this feature might not work properly on smartphones. Therefore you should use a native timer app on your phone until we've found a stable solution for this problem.

Chat

The Chat tab allows you to interact with other meditators.

Emojis

  • You can use Emojis by either using the emoji-menu (tag_faces) or manually inserting their codename in your message. A list of Emojis with their codes can be found here.

Questions

  • You have the option to mark your message as a question by either beginning the message with 'Q:' or using the Emoji :question: () anywhere inside your text. An admin then can click on your question and mark it as answered.
  • By clicking on you activate the QUESTIONS ONLY MODE. All messages that are not questions will be hidden.

Commitments

The Commit tab allows you to sign up for various commitments.

Profile

Clicking on a users avatar will load their Profile page.

Settings

  • You can select the information displayed for your profile by clicking on .
  • In your profile settings you can choose a default sound for your meditations and change your username, password, description and country.
  • In order to change your profile image, you need to register at Gravatar and set a Globally Recognized Avatar for the email adressed you've signed up with.

Appointments

The Appointments page is for those practicing formal meditation daily who would like to undertake a formal meditation course over the Internet. You'll find all necessary information on the page itself.

Testimonials

On the Testimonials page you can write down your experiences with your meditation practice. Please notice that your testimonial will be only displayed after it has been reviewed by an admin.

Livestream

A live broadcast or the time for the next one will be displayed on the Livestream page.
